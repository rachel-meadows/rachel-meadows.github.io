About me

Background

My background was originally in psychology. I was very interested in statistics and research, and did my honours thesis on how the visual system responds to fractal images.

For the last three years, I've worked as the Operations Manager of an eLearning startup. My role involved a lot of talking to users and translating their needs into tickets for the programmers. Then I got interested in programming myself, so I did a few online courses and started researching the career. Finally, I took the plunge to quit my job and enrol in Dev Academy!
